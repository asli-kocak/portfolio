Welcome to my website!

 As a computer science student at Tufts University, I've delved into both front-end and back-end programming, through various means such as TA'ing classes. Additionally, my passion for design led me to complement my studies with Engineering Psychology classes, allowing me to craft computer interfaces beyond the scope of my academic curriculum. This website serves as a platform to showcase a random selection of my recent work.

I am also co-majoring in Science Technology and Society Studies, because I am really interested in the impact that past technological developements have made in todays world, how and by whom new technologies and knowledge is being created, and what responsibilities and agencies we have as people who design, develop and manage technologies and products.

I am still at the beggining of my journey, finding my way through this field, so I'm looking forward to document my progress.
